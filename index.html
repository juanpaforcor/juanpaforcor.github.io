About


Hi there! I’m a passionate Software-Hardware Engineer with 10+ years of experience inventing, designing and engineering human-centered products at the frontier of technology.
My expertise blends software and hardware to create intuitive, human-centered solutions that leverage the latest in Extended Reality (XR), natural user interfaces, haptics, and AI.
With experience across academia, industry, and startups, I excel at turning ambitious visions into real-world products. From collaborating with medical professionals on life-saving monitoring devices to leading accessible edTech initiatives, I'm driven to maximize impact, anticipate trends, and open new avenues to enhance peoples' lives.
