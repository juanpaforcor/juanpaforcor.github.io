I am an electronics engineer and computer scientist with nine years of experience developing in-whole high-fidelity integrated solutions. I am currently the lead senior research engineer at the Augmented Human Lab (AHLAB) , The University of Auckland, led by Associate Proffesor Dr.Suranga Nanayakkara.
My professional career has provided me with ample exposure to industry, academia, and entrepreneurship. Through learning, creating, and innovating, my focus is to open up avenues to enhance peoples’ lives.

I completed my Master in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (First Class Honors) at the University of Auckland, New Zealand in 2020. I graduated with a B.Sc.(First Class Honors) in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from La Salle Engineering School - Ramon Llull University , Spain in 2013.
Since 2011 I have devoted my carrer to developing technological solutions in the fields of Human-Computer Interaction, Assistive Technologies, and Educational Technologies. I have worked in leading research institutions worldwide: Auckland Bioengineering Institute (ABI), Samsung Research America Think Tank Team, Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), Almende, Organizing Networks, and the Robotics and Engineering Department at La Salle University in Barcelona.
