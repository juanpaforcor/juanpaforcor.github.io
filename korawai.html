Korawai

A fusion of biotechnology, cultural heritage, and healthcare innovation

The Korawai Project seeks to improve health, well-being, and emotional connectivity in a rapidly changing world. Inspired by the Maori cultural ethos and leveraging cutting-edge haptics research, Korawai aims to address challenges in trauma recovery, chronic pain, and anxiety management.

Korawai develops wearable affective technologies that translate complex research concepts into tangible, market-viable products.

The Korawai Project garnered recognition and support within New Zealand's innovation ecosystem. Selection into the Kokiri accelerator program provided mentorship, networking opportunities, and seed startup funding. Further support from Callaghan Innovation included significant investment, underscoring the project's scientific rigor and market potential.

Korawai aligns with substantial economic opportunities in the elder care, pet tech, and agriculture markets, positioning it to meet the growing global demand for technological solutions in these areas.
